Notation guide
Format: [motif>r]n.p
ElementMeaning
[AAG]Canonical motif, no rotation
[AAG>1]Canonical rotated 1 position left: AAG → AGA
[~AAG]Reverse complement of canonical: AAG → CTT
[~AAG>2]RC of canonical rotated 2 left: AAG → GAA → TTC
]33 complete repeats
]3.13 complete repeats + 1 partial base
[N]55-base gap between stretches
[N]-22-base overlap between stretches
(ACGT)Literal gap sequence — shown when Full sequence in gaps is on
Options
OptionEffect

Strip prefix/suffix NSuppress only the leading and trailing [N] markers; keep internal ones
